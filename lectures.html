Dr. Yethindra has been an active participant in medical conferences, seminars, and workshops across the world. His lectures are known for their depth, clarity, and ability to break down complex medical concepts for broader audiences. Some of his notable presentations include:

Nipah Virus and Emerging Infectious Diseases

Presented at the Competition of Microbiology in Kyrgyzstan, 2018, where he discussed the importance of preparedness for viral outbreaks.

Understanding Alzheimer’s Disease

Presented at the Osmecon conference, where Dr. Yethindra detailed the various biological and psychosocial factors that contribute to Alzheimer's disease.

Rare Case of Secondary Tuberculosis of the Right Breast

At the AEGIS conference, Hyderabad, India, 2019, Dr. Yethindra presented a rare case focusing on tuberculosis mastitis and its diagnostic challenges.

Breast Cancer in Young Women

As a keynote speaker at the International Conference, Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan, 2019, Dr. Yethindra addressed biological, medical, and psychosocial challenges faced by young women with breast cancer.

Fluoxetine's Inhibition of SARS-CoV-2 in Vitro

At the LIMC in Lublin, Poland, 2020, Dr. Yethindra shared research on fluoxetine's potential antiviral effects as a repurposed treatment for COVID-19.

Insulin Resistance and Ventricular Remodeling in Hypertension

Presented at the 18th World Congress on Insulin Resistance, Diabetes & Cardiovascular Disease, Los Angeles, USA, 2020, Dr. Yethindra discussed how insulin resistance influences ventricular remodeling in patients with hypertension and prehypertension, highlighting its role in cardiovascular complications.

Tamoxifen and Doxorubicin Efficacy in Chemotherapy

At the International Conference for Healthcare and Medical Students (ICHAMS), Dublin, Netherlands, 2020, Dr. Yethindra evaluated the in vivo efficacy of different doses, formulations, and treatment schemes of tamoxifen and doxorubicin hydrochloride chemotherapy, providing insights into optimizing cancer treatment regimens.

Cardiovascular Disease and Diabetes as Predisposing Factors for COVID-19 Mortality

At the 5th Annual Heart in Diabetes Conference, New York, USA, 2021, Dr. Yethindra presented a systematic review and meta-analysis on how pre-existing cardiovascular diseases and diabetes mellitus increase mortality risks in patients with COVID-19.

Coenzyme Q10 Supplementation and Insulin Resistance in PCOS

Dr. Yethindra’s poster presentation at the 20th World Congress on Insulin Resistance, Diabetes & Cardiovascular Disease, Los Angeles, USA, 2022, explored the impact of coenzyme Q10 supplementation on improving insulin resistance in overweight/obese women with polycystic ovary syndrome.

Impact of Insulin Resistance and Inflammation on Vascular Rigidity in Hypertension and Fatty Liver Disease

At the 21st World Congress on Insulin Resistance, Diabetes & Cardiovascular Disease, California, USA, 2024, Dr. Yethindra discussed how insulin resistance and chronic low-grade inflammation contribute to vascular rigidity in patients with arterial hypertension and non-alcoholic fatty liver disease, highlighting their role in cardiovascular health.
