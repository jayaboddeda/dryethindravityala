Dr. Yethindra's trailblazing work in medical research has been recognized globally, earning him numerous accolades and honors.

Mahatma Gandhi National Award (2020)

Conferred by the Mahatma Gandhi Global Peace Forum in association with the United Nations, this award highlights Dr. Yethindra’s contributions to global healthcare and medical research.

Karmaveer Chakra Award (2021)

This prestigious honor, granted by iCONGO and the United Nations, celebrates his commitment to using medical science for social change and community health.

Champion of the Champions – MedEngage Award (2021)

Recognized by Metropolis Healthcare for his unparalleled achievements in medical research, particularly his contributions during the COVID-19 pandemic.

International Distinguished Young Researcher, Green Thinkerz, 2020

Awarded by Green Thinkerz in recognition of exceptional research accomplishments, highlighting innovative contributions to scientific knowledge and sustainable practices on a global platform.

Dr. Yellapragada Subbarao Memorial Award, Indian Association of Biomedical Scientists, 2022

Honored by the Indian Association of Biomedical Scientists for outstanding achievements in biomedical research, reflecting excellence in advancing healthcare and scientific discovery in the spirit of Dr. Yellapragada Subbarao's legacy.

Dr. Yethindra’s world records highlight his remarkable intellect and dedication to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in medical research and education. Some of his most notable records include:

Fastest Research Study Accomplished

In a remarkable achievement, Dr. Yethindra completed 20 advanced medical courses across 10 universities in just 9 days, further cementing his position as a medical prodigy.

Longest Title of a Book, Guinness World Records, 2020

Achieved a Guinness World Record for authoring a book with the longest title, showcasing creativity and dedication to unique literary contributions.

Youngest Scientist in Medicine

Awarded by multiple global organizations, including the World Record Certification Agency, this recognition celebrates Dr. Yethindra’s status as one of the youngest individuals to make groundbreaking contributions to medical science.

Youngest Person to Write a Trilingual Book, World Record Certification Agency, 2019

Became the youngest author to publish a book in three languages, demonstrating linguistic versatility and dedication to cross-cultural communication at an early age.

First Person in the World to Complete 20 Medical Courses at 10 Universities in 9 Days, World Record Certification Agency, 2019

Set a world record by completing 20 medical courses from 10 prestigious universities within just 9 days, exemplifying an extraordinary commitment to medical education.
